Common topics

Before you write

A few things we're asked most. If your question isn't here, email us anyway.

Setup & getting started

All apps

Our apps are players and tools — you connect them to a compatible service or account that you provide. If a connection fails, include the app name, your device, and the OS version when you write so we can help quickly.

Purchases & subscriptions

App Store · Google Play

In-app purchases and subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple or Google. Refunds and cancellations are handled through your App Store or Google Play account. We can help you locate the right setting.

Privacy & your data

Data requests

To ask what data we hold, or to request deletion, email us from the address tied to your account. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Report a problem

Bugs & feedback

Found a bug or have a request? Tell us what you expected and what happened. Screenshots and your app version help us reproduce it.
